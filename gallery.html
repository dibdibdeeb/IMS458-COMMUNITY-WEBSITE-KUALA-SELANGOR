Suggestion Route 2: LATAR Expressway

Using LATAR Expressway to Kuala Selangor, exit at Ijok, go to Kuala Selangor

Following is the four enter points to LATAR Expressway:

  1. Kabueng - Enter LATAR Expressway at Persiangan Templer - Exit at Ijok - Kuala Selangor
  2. Ijok Club - North Klang Valley Expressway via Persimpangan Rawang Selatan Exit 1.5 (Enter to Guthrie Corridor Expressway) - Enter to LATAR Express at Ijok - Kuala Selangor
  3. Shah Alam / Subang - Enter Guthrie Corridor Expressway - Enter to LATAR Express at Persimpangan Kapar Exit 2306 - Exit at Ijok - Kuala Selangor

Kuala Selangor.

Kuala Selangor is a town located in Selangor, Malaysia. "Kuala" means river mouth in the Malay language, and Kuala Selangor is a small town where the Selangor River (Sungai Selangor) meets the sea.
