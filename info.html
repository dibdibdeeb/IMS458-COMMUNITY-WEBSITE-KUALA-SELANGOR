Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Kuala Selangor located and hpw far is it from Kuala Lumpur?

Kuala Selangor is a small coastal town in the state of Selangor, Malaysia. It is located about 70-75 km from Kuala Lumpur and takes approximately 1 to 1.5 hours by car, depending on traffic. It is popular short gateway destination for city dwellers.

What are the must visit attractions in Kuala Selangor?

It is Bukit Melawati, Kampung Kuatan Fireflies Park, Sky Mirror, and Fresh Seafood Restaurants along the coast.

When is the best time to see fireflies?

Usually at night, between 7:30pm and 10:30pm. It is encouraged to come on a new moon or darker nights.

Are there accommodation options for visitors who want to stay overnight?

Yes, Kuala Selangor provides a budget hotels, homestays, guesthouses, and small resorts aas such at De Palma Resort Kuala Selangor, Sun Inns Hotel, Vi Boutique Hotel. and several cosy homestays around Kampung Kuantan and Pasir Penambang.

Kuala Selangor.

Kuala Selangor is a town located in Selangor, Malaysia. "Kuala" means river mouth in the Malay language, and Kuala Selangor is a small town where the Selangor River (Sungai Selangor) meets the sea.
